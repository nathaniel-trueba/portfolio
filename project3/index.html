Project 3: Exploring Global Temperature Trends - An Interactive Climate Visualization

Nidhi Manayilakath (nmanayilakath@ucsd.edu), Nathaniel Trueba (ntrueba@ucsd.edu), Tijil Chhabra (tchhabra@ucsd.edu)

Overview

How will Earth's average surface temperature evolve over the next century and how much does the outcome depend on the emissions choices we make today? This project uses CMIP6 multi-model climate projections to explore three Shared Socioeconomic Pathway (SSP) scenarios against the observed historical record. Both visualizations express temperature as an anomaly relative to the 1850–1900 pre-industrial baseline. Historical observations are drawn from the Berkeley Earth Land/Ocean Temperature Record. Projection endpoints follow IPCC multi-model medians for SSP1-2.6, SSP2-4.5 and SSP5-8.5.
